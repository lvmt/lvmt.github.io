Spatial omics integration · non-rigid registration (TPS) · large-scale visualization

Summary

I work at the intersection of spatial biology and geometric computation, with a focus on geometry-aware modeling for spatial omics integration, elastic / non-rigid alignment, and scalable interactive visualization.

I care about mathematical consistency and biological plausibility: alignment should be explainable, contours should be faithful, and deformation should be realistic rather than forced.

Core Strengths

Spatial Data + Geometry
  • Spatial transcriptomics / spatial metabolomics / single-cell workflows
  • Point clouds, meshes, volume rendering, and geometry processing
  • Contour modeling (e.g., concave hull / alpha shape) and robust fitting
Registration + Deformation
  • Affine + TPS (thin-plate spline) + non-rigid deformation modeling
  • Flow-field based control and cyclic / iterative alignment strategies
  • Sanity checks for overfitting and deformation artifacts
Engineering for Interactivity
  • React + deck.gl frontends for million-scale scatter visualization
  • FastAPI / Flask backends and compute-display separation
  • Dash-based interactive tools for alignment and inspection

What I Build

  • Interactive systems for large-scale spatial data visualization and registration
  • Geometry-aware alignment pipelines (contours, TPS, non-rigid deformation)
  • End-to-end tooling that bridges research prototypes and usable products
